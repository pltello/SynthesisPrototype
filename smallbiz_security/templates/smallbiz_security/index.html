Smallbiz Security

Cyber Security for Small Business



Who We Are

We are a nonprofit organization that helps small businesses prepare and protect themselves from cyber security attacks.

What We Do

We provide information and links to other resources about cyber security, common types of attacks, and planning and prevention. We collect data about organizations' systems, vulnerabilities, and cyber attacks to perform data anayltics to better help serve the small business community. We have a free version and a premium version to help cover the costs of running the non-profit. The paid version includes security audits and training.

Our Partnership

We partner with universities throughout the nation to assist small businesses and nonprofits with free security audits from university students, which began at West Texas A&M University in Texas. We use a lottery system to match students with local small businesses interested in free security audits. The data collected during these audits is reviewed. Any confidential or sensitive information is removed or redacted and then made available on our website for data analysis, data science, and machine learning projects.
